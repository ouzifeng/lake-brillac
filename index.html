A Warm Welcome To Lake Brillac. Poitou-Charente's Best Kept Carp Fishing Secret

Lake Brillac a private day ticket lake is situated on the edge of the village of Brillac in the beautiful Poitou-Charentes region of France. The lake is over 7acres and we limit to 10 anglers per day on a first come first served with a maximum 2 rods per angler (3 with arrangement or when possible)

Lake Brillac is situated only 10km north east of Confolens. We like to think the lake and is an ideal retreat for the dedicated carp, coarse or pole angler or if your just looking for a peaceful relaxing day out sat by the lake. We are open from 6am till late every day all year round (weather permitting)The cost for a day ticket is only 10 euros per angler per day, bait, coffee, tea ( and coming soon light snacks) are available at the lake.
